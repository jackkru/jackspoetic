# the climb

He glances up at the mountain<br>
His head sinks as he pushes on<br>
Every step a battle<br>
Every step a fight, an effort<br>
Up and up because<br>
He's scared to stop<br>
<br>
The rocks and shrubs<br>
Serve as his walking sticks<br>
As he pulls himself<br>
And pushes himself<br>
His mind is empty<br>
His heart is racing<br>
<br>
The men in front seem inevitable<br>
His breath is loud in his ears<br>
As he fights to keep up<br>
His heart is thumping<br>
And his head ringing<br>
Every step a battle<br>
<br>
His pack is heavy<br>
And pulling him back<br>
His fight grows harder<br>
Gravity is a mighty foe<br>
He needs to stop<br>
He wants to shout out<br>
<br>
And as he reaches for a tree<br>
He misses<br>
<br>
Shock, realization, and horror<br>
Bring his mind sharply into focus<br>
His mind has never moved faster<br>
Faster even than the fall<br>
So that when he stops<br>
He is calm
